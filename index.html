My Professional Journey

I was introduced to AI during my first internship at Buoy Health, where I worked with the company’s AI health assistant that provides personalized clinical support. Despite my initial unfamiliarity with artificial intelligence, collaborating closely with industry professionals sparked my enthusiasm for AI and its clear potential.

Subsequently, I worked as an undergraduate researcher at Yale University’s Zimmerman Lab, where I specialized in natural language processing. I utilized NLP models to analyze and compute topic distributions for scientific publications, aiding in the identification of nanomaterials for environmental applications.

Most recently, I completed an internship with Scale AI, where I worked with the latest ChatGPT model, training it on computer science and math-related tasks. My role involved conducting supervised learning by correcting the model's responses and creating complex coding and math problems to guide the model through solving them.
